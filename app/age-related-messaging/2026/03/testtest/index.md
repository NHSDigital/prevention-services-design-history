## Antenatal Screening Data Discovery

## Antenatal Screening Data Issues 

### The Problem

NHSE does not currently have a reliable, timely or detailed person-level view of data across the antenatal screening pathways. The data today is fragmented across various local systems and rooted in heavy reliance on manual processes. As a result, it is difficult to produce reports which confidently assure safety, identify emerging risks or inequalities, or understand where and why pathway breakdowns occur.
